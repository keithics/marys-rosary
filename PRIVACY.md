# Privacy Policy

**Mary's Rosary** is a completely offline app. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data — ever.

## What we collect

Nothing.

## Network access

The app makes no network requests. All prayers, audio, and content are bundled inside the app and work without an internet connection.

## Third-party services

None. There are no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, advertising frameworks, or third-party integrations of any kind.

## Data stored on your device

The app saves only your preferences and session state (current mystery, bead position, delay settings) locally on your device using iOS standard storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Children

This app is safe for all ages. No data is collected from anyone, including children.
